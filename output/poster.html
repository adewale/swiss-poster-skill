03 / The Abuse/03

RANSOM
NOTE.

Once anyone with a Mac Plus and a LaserWriter could mix 35 typefaces in a single document, they did. Every word a different font, weight, and size — like a kidnapper's note. The deluge had names, all of them deserved. Robin Williams's The Mac Is Not a Typewriter (1990) and Spiekermann's Stop Stealing Sheep (1993) were partly written as field manuals against it.
