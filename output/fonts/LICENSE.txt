URW Base35 fonts (free clones of the PostScript 35)
====================================================

Source: https://github.com/ArtifexSoftware/urw-base35-fonts
License: GNU Affero General Public License v3 (AGPL-3.0) with font
exception, OR The URW++ License — see the upstream repo for full text.

These OTF files are URW++'s free re-releases of their PostScript 35
clones, donated to the GhostScript / Artifex project in 2009 and
re-released as free software in 2015. They are functional clones of:

  NimbusRoman-Regular.otf      — Times Roman
  NimbusSans-Regular.otf       — Helvetica
  NimbusSansNarrow-Regular.otf — Helvetica Narrow
  NimbusMonoPS-Regular.otf     — Courier
  URWGothic-Book.otf           — ITC Avant Garde Gothic
  URWBookman-Light.otf         — ITC Bookman
  C059-Roman.otf               — New Century Schoolbook (Century 751)
  P052-Roman.otf               — Palatino (URW Palladio)
  Z003-MediumItalic.otf        — ITC Zapf Chancery (URW Chancery)
  StandardSymbolsPS.otf        — Symbol
  D050000L.otf                 — ITC Zapf Dingbats (Dingbats)

Used in this repo solely as typographic specimens within a poster.
