two ways to use the same frame only one of them is Cynefin
A — Sorting
SORTING
the model shapes the data
You bring the boxes. Data gets filed into categories fixed in advance. Fast, repeatable, and blind to whatever has no slot.
every cell stamped
before you look
B — Sensing
SENSING
the data shapes the model
You bring attention. The pattern shows itself first; the category comes after, and stays provisional. Slower, and honest about what you don't yet know.
one mark surfaces
the line drawn after
the test
A 2×2 you fill in is a sorting box. Cynefin earns its place only when the situation, not the diagram, picks the domain.
Use it backwards — choose the domain you'd prefer, then gather evidence to fit — and it becomes the bias it was built to expose.
after Hofmann & Ruder · figure, ground, function
Sense first. Sort never.
