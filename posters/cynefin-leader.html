the move, and the trap

A decision sheet · four domains · four leaders
Lead the domain you're in,
not the one you're good at.
Domain
The move
The trap
01
Clear
Ordered
The move
Set the rule, delegate, communicate plainly.
↯ The trap
Complacency. You stop checking, and the world changes under the rule.
02
Complicated
Ordered
The move
Convene experts, weigh conflicting advice, decide.
↯ The trap
Paralysis, or experts who wave away the answer they didn't think of.
03
Complex
Unordered
The move
Run safe-to-fail probes, raise interaction, let patterns emerge.
↯ The trap
Demanding certainty up front, and managing it like it's merely complicated.
04
Chaotic
Unordered
The move
Act now to make order, then move toward complex.
↯ The trap
Staying the commander after the crisis, when the room is ready to learn.
the danger
Each domain rewards a different kind of leader. The danger is bringing last year's strength to this year's problem.
