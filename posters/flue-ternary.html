The harness
trilemma.

A harness optimizes for three things at once — and trades them off. Plot Flue, Cloudflare's Project Think and Vercel's Eve on the triangle: each leans toward a different corner.
Nearer a corner ▸ stronger on that trait
Common ground All three are open-source TypeScript durable runtimes — they just optimize for different corners.
Flue leans portability · Think leans platform power · Eve leans batteries-included.
