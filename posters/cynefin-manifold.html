René Thom's cusp: a smooth control plane below, a pleated response surface above. Inside the cusp the system has two stable states; push past the edge and it jumps to the other — no middle, no warning. That jump is Clear falling into Chaos.
On the flat part you can edge along, adjusting as you go. Over the fold there is no edging — the state you were in stops existing and you drop to the only one left.
