Why it holds

Secrets live outside the sandbox. Handlers run in the Workers runtime, so they hold tokens the sandbox can never read.

HTTPS is inspected safely. An ephemeral CA is minted per instance; its private key never leaves the sidecar.

Deny by default. An allowlist of hosts is permitted; everything else is blocked at egress.

Policy is hot-swappable. setOutboundHandler() adds or removes rules at runtime — no sandbox restart.

Everything is auditable. Each subrequest lands in a tamper-evident, hash-chained log.
